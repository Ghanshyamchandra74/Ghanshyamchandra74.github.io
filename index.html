Research

I'm interested in Mathematical Modeling of Fluids Flows coupled with Reinforcement learning, optimization, and High Performance Computing. Much of my research is about Optimizing the Numerical Schemes to leverage High Performance Computing (Heterogeneous Architectures, Accelerators, multiNode:multiGPU Solvers, Parallel Visualisations) and Optimal Parameter Estimation for Deterministic and Stochastic PDE's with Deep Reinforcement learning algorithms.
